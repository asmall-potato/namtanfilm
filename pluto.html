ABOUT Pluto Series

​​"Pluto Series" is GMMTV's second sapphire series, starring by Namtan Tipnaree and Film Rachanun.
The series is adapted from the novel "PLUTO: A Story, A Planet, and Love" written by Chao Planoy.
